Mobile UX

Write for mobile first - it is the best way to ensure that the simplicity remains in your program.
Most users will have mobile and will go there first and you want them to have a good first impression.
Keep menus in easy to reach locations 
Make things that matter quick.  TIME IS MONEY to consumers
SVG/Webfonts and optimize and Minify your CSS and JS
Minimize forms, if needed paginate to reduce intimidation
Keep ads non-invasive.  NEVER USE POPUPS or POPOVERS
Make content the priority on the page
